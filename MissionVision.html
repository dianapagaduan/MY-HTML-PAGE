VISION

PUP Santa Maria envisions to offer the students of the municipality quality, relevant,
responsive, and excellent tertiary education aimed to produce graduates that are
future leaders and expert in their chosen field of endeavor and contributes
to the full development of the family, community, society and the nation at large.



MISSION

To provide high quality undergraduate programs and globally-competitive human capital
development and community services accessible particularly to the students of the municipality
