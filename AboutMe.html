ABOUT ME

Hello there! My name is Diana Pagaduan, and I'm an IT student with a deep passion for all things related to graphics design and art.
I believe that creativity knows no bounds, and I constantly strive to push the limits of my imagination.

As someone who always seeking new avenues of self-expression, I find immense joy in exploring various art forms and experimenting
with different techniques. Whether it's digital design, photo editing, or even photography, I love immersing myself in the world of visuals
and creating works that evoke emotions and tell stories.

My main goal is to finish school and get a degree in Information Technology. I'm motivated to provide myself with the skills and abilities
required to succeed in the creative industry because education is essential to shaping one's future. Beyond that,
I would like to have an impact on the field of design by producing pieces that touch and inspire individuals from every walk of life.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me better. I invite you to explore my gallery and witness the passion and creativity
that I pour into my work. Let's connect and explore this artistic journey together!
